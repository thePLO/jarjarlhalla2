JAR! JARL! HALLA!!

Hi Vikingks!!

After a great day of sailing and raiding, there's only one last thing to do: Chug down all the booze you can!

"Jar! Jarl! Halla!" is an arcade score attack in which you will have to keep giving alcohol to all the thirsty vikings while dodging attacks from the ones that have had one too many.

Keep them served or a fight might break out and ruin your tavern (again) !

Controls:
  • Left and Right to get close to the tables
  • Spacebar while close to a table to server a horn
